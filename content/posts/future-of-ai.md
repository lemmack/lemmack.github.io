Artificial Intelligence is evolving rapidly. The shift from chat-based interfaces to agentic workflows is transforming how we interact with computers.

## What are Agents?

Agents are AI systems capable of performing actions, not just generating text. They can:
- Execute code
- Browse the web
- Manage files
